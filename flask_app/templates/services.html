Withdrawals:

During the blood test, the patient is taken care of by an authorized health professional.

The time slot (except for certain examinations) recommended for blood samples is in the morning before 11 a.m. Depending on the prescribed examinations, certain specific conditions must be met. Do not hesitate to inquire with the laboratory.


Fasting :

A strict fast of 8 to 12 hours (do not eat anything, do not smoke and drink only non-carbonated water only) is necessary for the realization of the glycemia or a lipid balance (cholesterol, triglycerides, HDL and LDL )


Diet and lifestyle :

Dietary and even professional habits are important to know in order to interpret certain results: night work, tobacco consumption and even diet can influence the examinations to be carried out.

Bacteriological, parasitological and mycological samples:

In general, it is desirable that you respect the recommendations following:
. Perform the examination away from any local or general antibiotic or anti-infective treatment (at least 48 hours)
. Do not wash the areas to be sampled in the laboratory (secretions or pus)
. Identify any samples brought with their surname, first name, date of birth, date and time of collection on the label
. Hermetically close the bottle and place it in an airtight bag.
