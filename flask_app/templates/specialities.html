Specialities


Biochemistry


Biochemistry is the determination of molecules contained in body fluids (blood, cerebrospinal fluid, urine, etc.) that may be involved in a pathology.


Hormonology


Hormonal assays make it possible to participate in the diagnosis of endocrine dysfunctions and to monitor the evolution of its pathologies and their treatment. Marker assays make it possible to diagnose and monitor the evolution of tumor pathologies.



Hemostase


Hemostasis corresponds to all the physiological phenomena to stop bleeding in case of rupture of a blood vessel


Immunology


Infectious serology: Infectious serology is used to detect and measure antibodies and/or anitgens specific to certain infectious agents, which makes it possible to diagnose infections.


Allergology


Allergy is an abnormal and specific reaction of an organism in contact with a foreign substance (allergen),these foreign substances do not cause disorders in most subjects. The identification of allergens responsible for allergic manifestations is an essential step to identify the allergic nature of clinical manifestations observed in a patient and thus implement the appropriate therapeutic strategy. Our examinations consist of a possible detection coupled with a quantification of serum specific IgE
